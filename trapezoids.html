THE TRAPEZIUS MUSCLE

The trapezius muscle is a large triangular muscle in the upper back and neck.It`s crusial for shoulder and neck movements.Divided into upper,middle and lower regions,it helps elevate,retract and depress the shoulder blades.Connected by the accessory nerve,it plays a key role in maintaining posture and shoulder stability.

Exersises that stimulate the trapezius muscles

DUMBELL SHRUGS

  • SETUP
    • Stand up straight with a dumbell in each hand,arms fully extended and palms facing your body
  • SHRUGGING
    • Lift your shoulders as high as possible towards your ears.Focus on lifting your shoulders straight up,avoiding any forward or backward movement
  • HOLD THE CONTRACTION
    • At the top of the shrug,squeeze your traps and hold the contraction for a moment to maximize the muscle engagement
  • LOWERING
    • Slowly lower the dumbells back down to the starting position
  • REPEAT THE MOVEMENT

FACE PULLS

  • STARTING POSITION
    1. Position yourself so that your body is upright and your arms are fully extended in front of you
    2. Your palms should be facing each other and your elbows should be slightly bent
  • EXECUTION
    1. Pull the rope towards your face by externally rotating your shoulders.
    2. Focus on pulling the rope apart and aiming for the sides of your face.
    3. Lead the movement with your elbows, bringing them out to the sides.
    4. Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the end of the movement.
    5. Ensure that your upper arms are parallel to the ground or slightly above.
  • RETURN
    1. Slowly extend your arms back to the starting position, controlling the resistance.
  • REPEAT
