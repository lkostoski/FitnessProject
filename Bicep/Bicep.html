THE BICEP MUSCLE

EXCERCISES THAT SPECIALIZE ON MUSCLE GROWTH IN THE BICEP AREA

DUMBBELL CURL

  • SETUP
    • To do a biceps curl with a dumbbell, hold a dumbbell with your palm facing upward.
  • HOW TO EXECUTE
    1. Slowly curl the weight up by bending your elbow, keeping your elbow close to your body.
    2. . Then slowly lower the weight to the starting position. You'll feel tension in the muscles in the front of your upper arm.

HAMMER CURL

  • STARTING POSITION
    • Stand with good posture and dumbbells by your sides, so the ends of them point forwards, like holding a hammer in each hand.
  • HOW TO PERFORM
    • Raise the right dumbbell up to your right shoulder, keep you elbow tucked at your side then lower it.
    • Repeat on the left arm and keep alternating arms for the duration of the exercise.
